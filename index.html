ABOUT US

Designed by Gerald C. Roby in 1966 High Point is an outstanding 18 hole championship course located at the High Point Golf Club facility in Montague, NJ. From the longest tees it offers 6,783 yards of golf for a par of 73.

Located in Montague, NJ High Point Golf Club has 18 picturesque holes that are surrounded by crystal lakes and are a challenge to play for both professional and amateur golfers.

With its immaculate greens, lush landscaping and views of the High Point monument from the golf course, within a year of re-opening High Point Golf Club had proven itself to be one of the finest courses in the area.

With scenic views of Holiday Lake, the Blue Heron Bar & Grill offer diners a wide variety of dishes as well as a full bar. Upstairs you will find an eloquent ballroom with floor to ceiling windows and exquisite views that are perfect for hosting any event.
